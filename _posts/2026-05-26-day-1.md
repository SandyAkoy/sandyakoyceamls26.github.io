what_i_learned: |

   Today marked the official launch and orientation for the Artificial Intelligence Program at Morgan State University, setting a strong foundational tone for the rest of the summer. During the orientation sessions, I met my assigned research team and connected with the broader cohort while learning about the core structure, academic expectations, and overarching mission of the SAIRI program. We covered essential topics in artificial intelligence, setting clear benchmarks for the technical rigor and collaborative mindset required for our upcoming projects. The leadership team provided valuable insights into how we will navigate our research modules, outlining the tools, ethical frameworks, and computational methodologies we will be deploying. It was inspiring to see the scope of what we will be building, and the orientation gave me a clear sense of direction as we begin this journey.

blockers: |

  This was my very first time using GitHub, navigating the platform, managing repositories, and understanding the step-by-step workflow felt quite tedious and overwhelming at first. Learning how version control functions, alongside the initial setup process, required extra patience and careful attention to detail. However, despite the steep learning curve, I am proud of how I handled my first real exposure to the platform, and I am looking forward to getting much more comfortable with coding and managing repositories on GitHub as the program progresses.

reflection: |

   Overall, today was an incredible, insight-filled start to the program that left me feeling motivated and deeply encouraged. Meeting my peers during team introductions helped build an instant sense of community, making the environment feel welcoming and supportive right from day one. Although the schedule was packed with information and could have easily felt overwhelming, the engaging sessions made the time fly by surprisingly fast. The icebreaker bingo game was a particular highlight because it gave us a fun, interactive way to learn about each other on a deeper, more personal level beyond just our academic backgrounds. I am leaving day one feeling excited about the team dynamic we are building and truly eager to dive deeper into our AI research in the days ahead
