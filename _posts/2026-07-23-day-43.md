   Today was a high-stakes, transformative milestone in our summer research journey as we delivered our presentation in front of the official SAIRI staff. Presenting the culmination of our summer work to the leadership team was a thrilling and nerve-wracking experience, but our team stood firm and delivered a comprehensive breakdown of our cardiovascular AI research. Performance Benchmarks: highlighting our optimized Drift Classification Accuracy (DCA), Energy Drift Correlation (EDC), and peak Complementary Reliability Gain (CRG) verified across the PTB-XL, BIDMC, and MIMIC datasets. Clinical Safety & Robustness: presenting our Trust Hierarchy Validation, PPG Failure Mode Analysis, and the strategic use of our reserved respiratory channel as a dynamic tiebreaker during signal corruptions. Following our presentation, Dr. Mack, Elijah, and Michael provided us with detailed, constructive critiques on our content, slide flow, and delivery. Immediately after the session ended, our group met to break down their feedback and outline exact revisions to apply for our final official presentation this coming Sunday. I then spent the remainder of the day running through my individual slides, practicing my timing, vocal delivery, and slide transitions to ensure my parts are completely flawless.
