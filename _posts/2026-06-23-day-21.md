    Today marked a major leap forward in the research process for our model as we validated several critical performance and safety metrics simultaneously. Following these great data results, the biggest breakthrough of the day was calculating the complementarity reliability gain CRG. This directly supports our core hypothesis: multimodal complementary fusion improves overall model reliability by drastically reducing redundancy. We are effectively using these proven datasets to fuel an experiment that will ultimately predict cardiovascular diseases using these distinct biosignals. Today was a day of overcoming hurdles and using concrete results to validate our choices, whilst simultaneously gathering more research for our literature review papers.
